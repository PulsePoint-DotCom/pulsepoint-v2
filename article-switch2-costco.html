Nintendo Switch 2 Games Are Having a Surprise Sale at Costco

April 19, 2026 · 3 min read

By Zara Kim · Viral Editor

Something weird happened at Costco over the weekend. Reports started flooding in from gamers who spotted Nintendo Switch 2 titles — brand new releases — marked down significantly below retail price. We're talking $10-20 off on games that came out weeks ago.

Wait, Is This Real?

Yes and no. Multiple gamers on Reddit and Twitter posted photos of Switch 2 games at their local Costco with yellow clearance-style stickers. But it's not a chain-wide thing — it seems to be individual locations clearing early inventory. Think of it as the warehouse store version of finding a good deal at a weird time.

🛒 What to look for: The discounts appear focused on games like the new Pokemon titles and Mario Kart launch releases — the ones that sold well initially but might have overstock. If you've been eyeing a Switch 2 game and have a Costco membership, it might be worth a drive-by your local warehouse.

The Nintendo Switch 2 launched last month to massive demand — most models are still selling out within hours of restocks. So a surprise sale is unexpected and has the internet buzzing. Is Nintendo seeing weaker than expected demand? Are they clearing inventory for a new wave? Nobody's saying officially.

Why This Matters

For the past year, "Switch 2" has been synonymous with "impossible to find." Scalpers were charging double or triple retail. This Costco sighting is the first sign that the supply situation might finally be catching up to demand. That's good news if you're looking to buy — and it might mean you won't need to set your alarm for 3 AM waiting for a Nintendo Direct restock alert.

Check your Costco before you pay full price anywhere else. And if you're not a member, borrow a friend's card — this deal is worth the awkward ask.
